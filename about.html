About Red Gorilla Production

Red Gorilla Production is a young full-service film and video production company based in the Philippines and set to infect the world with its zeal for visual storytelling and moving picture

With experience ranging from end to end of the vast production spectrum, its founders draw from unique, individual backgrounds in order to produce a portfolio held together by impeccable taste, impressive skill, and world-class professionalism.

Though significantly experienced in commercial and corporate work, Red Gorilla Production specializes in shooting food, travel, and lifestyle videos.

Always longing for a challenge, the company's founders take part in coming up with great audio-visual solutions to a myriad of commercial, corporate, artistic, and personal ideas.
